About us

Rehoboth construction logistic SARL has established itself as a contractor of choice in the mining sector of the Katanga copper belt region, and a renowned partner of the university sector across the Democratic Republic of Congo. Our primary objective is to satisfy the needs of our customers by offering tailor-made solutions and services that improve their productivity, their competitive position, and increase their long-term business success. We are constantly striving to find ways to take your business to the next level. At Rehoboth construction logistic SARL, professionalism, integrity, excellence, customer satisfaction and social empowerment are our core values.
